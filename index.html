If you believe that Nepal is only mountains and Mount Everest, then you're completely wrong. Nepal, especially when travelling with Exploring Nepal, is much, much more than that.If you want to ride a motorbike to the places that are not displayed on the map – it's possible. If you are a nature observer, willing to see rare bird species, such as Himalayan Monal, that's possible too.If you're a musician/artist willing to perform and looking for a venue – that also can be sorted.If you are interested in religions, and want to discover secrets of buddism and hinduism – it will be arranged. Even swimming in the mountain river, without a boat or pontoon behind the stones – can be a part of your journey.And obviously, if you (only) want to go on a trek with an experienced guide and brave porter who will help you anyway you want – that will also be organised.A couple more things are available, but we will not go that far here – all these things, without any problem can be provided and organised by Magda and Sujan (with their great team) from Exploring Nepal.
